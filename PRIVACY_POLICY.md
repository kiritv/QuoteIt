# Privacy Policy - Quote It

**Effective date:** April 26, 2026

Quote It ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how the Quote It mobile application (the "App") handles information.

## Summary

- Quote It does not require account creation.
- Most Quote It activity is local to your device.
- We do not sell your personal data.
- Some optional features send limited information to QuoteIt services or third parties. These include Creator Assist, remote dataset discovery/downloads, web background images, and push notification registration.

## Information Stored On Your Device

Quote It stores app data locally on your device. Depending on the features you use, this can include:

- favorites
- collections
- reading position and settings
- journal entries, mood check-ins, and streak/progress data
- creator drafts and creator workspace state
- imported datasets and related metadata
- downloaded background images and selected photo backgrounds
- locally selected or recorded media used in creator flows
- Premium status cache and related app settings

This local data is not uploaded to us unless you use a feature that explicitly relies on a network service.

## Information Sent To QuoteIt Services

### Creator Assist

Creator Assist is an optional feature. When Creator Assist uses cloud generation or web context, the App may send data to a QuoteIt-managed backend. This can include:

- selected text or current draft text
- your prompt or refinement request
- the requested output type
- bounded source metadata such as quote ID, source name, or author
- limited context needed to generate a response
- app and device metadata such as install ID, request ID, app version, build number, locale, preferred language, region, time zone, and OS version
- Premium entitlement proof used to verify access to paid cloud generation

We use this information to generate Creator Assist responses, enforce Premium entitlement and usage limits, maintain service reliability, and help prevent abuse.

If web context is enabled for Creator Assist, the QuoteIt backend may also call external search or model providers on your behalf. In those cases, the request may include the limited text and context needed to produce the result.

### Push Notification Registration

If you allow notifications and the build is configured for QuoteIt push services, the App may register your device with a QuoteIt backend. This registration can include:

- APNs device token
- install ID
- app version and build number
- locale, preferred language, region, and time zone
- device model, device model code, device name, and OS version
- push environment and related registration metadata

We use this information only to register your device for notifications, deliver notifications, and maintain notification infrastructure.

## Information Sent To Third Parties

### Background Images

If you enable web background images, Quote It may request images from Unsplash and/or Pexels. Those providers may receive standard network information such as your IP address, device metadata, and request details needed to return the image.

Their privacy policies are:

- Unsplash: https://unsplash.com/privacy
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/privacy/

### Remote Dataset Discovery And Downloads

If you browse or install remote datasets, Quote It may contact QuoteIt-hosted delivery infrastructure, GitHub-hosted resources, or related content endpoints to fetch catalog metadata and dataset packages. Those services may receive standard network information needed to complete the request.

### Apple Services

Apple may process information when you use Apple-provided platform services in the App, including:

- StoreKit purchases, subscriptions, and restore flows
- push notifications through APNs
- photo library access
- speech recognition
- camera or microphone access

Those services are governed by Apple's policies and platform terms.

## What Stays Local

The following flows are designed to stay on-device unless you explicitly use a network-backed feature:

- reading and browsing bundled content
- favorites, collections, journal entries, and most settings
- local creator drafts and edits
- imported local files and datasets
- selected Photos backgrounds and locally recorded media
- video export and local audio mixing

Quote It does not upload your imported files, local media, or journal content by default.

## Permissions

Quote It may request the following permissions depending on the features you use:

- **Notifications:** to schedule and deliver daily or related notifications
- **Photo Library (Read):** to let you select photos or videos for creator backgrounds and media import
- **Photo Library (Add Only):** to save generated images or videos to your photo library
- **Camera:** to capture photos or record video inside creator flows
- **Microphone:** to record narration or use dictation features
- **Speech Recognition:** to convert spoken input to text for dictation features

You can decline permissions, and many parts of the App will continue to work without them.

## Data Sharing And Selling

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not require a user account to use the App.
- We share information only as needed to operate the optional services described in this policy.

## Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above and publish the revised policy.

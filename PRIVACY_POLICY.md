# Privacy Policy – Quote It

**Effective date:** February 17, 2026

Quote It ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how the Quote It mobile application (the "App") handles information.

## Summary
- We do **not** collect, store, or sell personal data.
- The App works offline with a bundled quote dataset.
- If you enable background images, the App may contact image providers (Unsplash/Pexels) to download images.
- Optional notifications are handled on-device.

## Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect or process:
- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or identifiers
- Precise location
- Contacts, photos, or files (except when you choose to save an image to Photos)
- Health or financial data
- Usage analytics or advertising identifiers

## Information Processed On Device
The App stores certain data locally on your device:
- Favorites
- Notification preferences
- Cached background images (if enabled)

This data is stored locally and is not transmitted to us.

## Background Images (Optional)
If background images are enabled, the App may request images from Unsplash and/or Pexels. These services may receive standard network information (such as your IP address and device metadata) as part of the request. We do not receive or store this information.

Unsplash and Pexels have their own privacy policies:
- Unsplash: https://unsplash.com/privacy
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/privacy/

## Permissions
- **Notifications (Optional):** Used only to schedule daily quote notifications.
- **Photos (Add Only):** Used only when you choose to save a quote image to your Photos library.

## Children’s Privacy
The App is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated effective date.
